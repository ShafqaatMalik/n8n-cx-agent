# VoltShop Return & Refund Policy

## Standard Returns
VoltShop offers a 30-day return window from the date of delivery. Items must be in original packaging, unused, and include all accessories. To initiate a return, customers need their order number and the email address used at checkout.

## Refund Processing
Once we receive the returned item, refunds are processed within 5-7 business days. Refunds are issued to the original payment method. Customers receive an email confirmation when the refund is processed.

## Refund Amounts
Full refunds are provided for defective products or incorrect shipments. For change-of-mind returns, a restocking fee of 10% applies. Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.

## Non-Returnable Items
The following items cannot be returned: opened software, gift cards, clearance items marked as final sale, and items with physical damage caused by the customer. Custom-built PCs are non-returnable but are covered under warranty.

## Damaged or Defective Items
If you receive a damaged or defective product, contact us within 48 hours of delivery. We will arrange a free return pickup and send a replacement or issue a full refund — your choice. Photos of the damage are required for processing.

## Late Returns
Returns requested after the 30-day window are handled on a case-by-case basis. We may offer store credit instead of a refund. Contact our support team to discuss options.
