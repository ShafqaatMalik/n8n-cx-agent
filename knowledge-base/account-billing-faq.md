# VoltShop Account & Billing FAQ

## Creating an Account
You can create a VoltShop account at voltshop.com/register. An account is not required to make a purchase, but registered users get access to order history, faster checkout, and exclusive member deals.

## Payment Methods
We accept the following payment methods:
- Credit/Debit cards: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
- PayPal
- Apple Pay and Google Pay
- Afterpay (buy now, pay in 4 installments — available for orders $50-$1,000)
- VoltShop Gift Cards

## Billing Issues
If you see an unexpected charge, check your order history first — it may be from a pending pre-order or a shipping surcharge. If the charge is still unexplained, contact support with the charge amount, date, and last 4 digits of your card. We will investigate within 24 hours.

## Canceling an Order
Orders can be canceled within 1 hour of placement if they haven't entered processing. After that, the order must be returned once delivered. To cancel, contact support immediately with your order number.

## Gift Cards
VoltShop gift cards are available in $25, $50, $100, and $200 denominations. Gift cards never expire and are non-refundable. They can be used for any product on voltshop.com. Gift card balances cannot be converted to cash.

## Taxes
Sales tax is calculated based on the shipping address and applicable state/local tax rates. Tax amounts are shown at checkout before payment is submitted.

## Promotional Codes
Promo codes can be applied at checkout. Only one promo code can be used per order. Promo codes cannot be combined with the price match guarantee. Codes are case-sensitive and have expiration dates listed in the promotional email.
