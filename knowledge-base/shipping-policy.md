# VoltShop Shipping Policy

## Shipping Methods & Timeframes
- **Standard Shipping**: 5-7 business days. Free on orders over $75.
- **Express Shipping**: 2-3 business days. Flat rate $12.99.
- **Next-Day Shipping**: Next business day if ordered before 2 PM EST. $24.99.

## Order Processing
Orders are processed within 1 business day. Orders placed on weekends or holidays are processed the next business day. You will receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking number once your order ships.

## Tracking Your Order
After your order ships, use the tracking number in your confirmation email to check status on our website or the carrier's site. Standard orders ship via USPS or FedEx Ground. Express and Next-Day orders ship via FedEx.

## Shipping Restrictions
We do not have any branches/outlets except the United States. We currently ship to the continental United States only. We do not ship to PO Boxes for Express or Next-Day orders. Oversized items (TVs 65"+, gaming desks) may incur an additional $15 shipping surcharge.

## Delivery destinations
Unfortunately, VoltShop only delivers within the continental United States. We do not currently ship internationally or to US territories outside the continental US. If you are located outside the US, we recommend checking with a US-based freight forwarding service that can ship internationally on your behalf.

## Delivery Issues
If your package shows as delivered but you haven't received it, wait 24 hours as carriers sometimes mark packages early. If still missing, contact us with your order number and we will file a carrier investigation. For packages lost in transit, we will reship the order or issue a full refund.

## Changing Your Delivery Address
Address changes can only be made before your order has been shipped. Once a shipping label has been created and the package is with the carrier, we are unable to modify the delivery address.

**If your order has not yet shipped:**
Contact our support team as soon as possible with your order number and the corrected address. Since orders are processed within 1 business day, you typically have a short window to request this change. We cannot guarantee the update will be applied in time, but we will do our best.

**If your order has already shipped:**
We cannot change the address once the package is in transit. You have two options:
- **Intercept/redirect via the carrier**: For FedEx shipments (Express and Next-Day orders), you may be able to request a redirect directly through FedEx Delivery Manager at no cost, depending on the shipment status. For USPS shipments, you can use USPS Package Intercept (fees may apply).
- **Refuse or return the package**: If the package is delivered to the old address, you can refuse delivery or arrange a return. Once we receive it back, we will reship to the correct address. Standard return shipping times apply.

VoltShop is not responsible for packages delivered to an incorrect address provided at checkout. Please double-check your shipping address before placing your order.

## Holiday Shipping
During holiday seasons (Nov 15 - Dec 31), standard shipping may take up to 10 business days due to carrier volume. We recommend Express or Next-Day shipping for time-sensitive gifts.
