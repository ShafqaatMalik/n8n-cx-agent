# VoltShop Technical Support FAQ

## Laptop Won't Turn On
First, ensure the laptop is charged — connect the power adapter and wait 15 minutes before trying again. If the power LED doesn't light up, try a different outlet. If it still won't turn on, hold the power button for 15 seconds to perform a hard reset. If the issue persists, contact support for a warranty claim.

## Monitor Has Dead Pixels
VoltShop follows the manufacturer's dead pixel policy. Most manufacturers consider 3 or fewer dead pixels within spec. If your monitor has more than 3 dead pixels, or has any dead pixels in the center region, contact us within 30 days for a replacement under our return policy.

## Slow Performance Issues
If your VoltShop product is running slowly, try these steps:
1. Restart the device
2. Check for OS and driver updates
3. Close unnecessary background applications
4. For laptops, ensure the power plan is set to "High Performance"
If the issue persists after these steps, contact our support team. For custom PC buyers, we offer a free remote diagnostic session within the first 6 months.
