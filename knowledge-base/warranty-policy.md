# VoltShop Warranty Policy

## Standard Warranty
All products sold by VoltShop include the manufacturer's warranty. Warranty periods vary by product category:
- Laptops & Desktops: 1 year
- Monitors & Displays: 2 years
- Headphones & Audio: 1 year
- Cables & Accessories: 6 months
- Custom-Built PCs: 2 years (VoltShop warranty)

## VoltShop Extended Warranty (VoltShield)
For an additional fee, customers can purchase VoltShield extended warranty at checkout:
- VoltShield 1-Year Extension: 12% of product price
- VoltShield 2-Year Extension: 20% of product price
VoltShield covers manufacturing defects, hardware failures, and battery degradation beyond 20%. It does not cover accidental damage, water damage, or software issues.

## How to File a Warranty Claim
1. Contact VoltShop support with your order number and a description of the issue.
2. Our team will troubleshoot remotely when possible.
3. If a replacement is needed, we will ship a replacement and provide a prepaid return label for the defective item.
4. Warranty replacements are processed within 3-5 business days after we receive the defective item.

## What Voids the Warranty
- Unauthorized modifications or repairs
- Physical damage from drops, spills, or misuse
- Use of incompatible third-party accessories that cause damage
- Removal or tampering with serial number labels
